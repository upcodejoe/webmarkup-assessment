AI Match Analytics

The competition in sports industry is massive nowadays. Matches are getting attention from millions of people. Also there are strict competition in the sports betting industry leads to razor thin profit margins. Therefore every single betting company would like to offer highest odds to acquire new customers but also to have enough of profit margins. That is when AI might come in the play. With the help of well trained AI models, betting companies can track hundreds of matches at the same time. They can analize and track all aspects of the match live on-time and adjust their odds according to data.

AI Health Coaching

The sports industry today is worth dozens of billions. Therefore high performance athletes getting paid big numbers. But the big paycheck comes with the big responsibility. They have to make sure that their body is in the best potential. They also have to protect themselves from injuries. Because a single injury before a very critical match can cause the team billions of dollars. Today many sports clubs started to use the power of AI to provide their athletes with better health coaching so to make sure they are at their best potential and they stay away from injuries.
